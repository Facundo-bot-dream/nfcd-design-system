Wide-tracked uppercase mono kicker that sits above titles and labels metadata — the brand's signature small-type voice.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Encuentro · Otoño 2026</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow tone="muted">Campo Arroyo del Medio</Eyebrow>
```

Tones: `wine` (default), `ink`, `muted`, `onInk` (for dark grounds). Keep text short; it is always uppercased and heavily tracked.
