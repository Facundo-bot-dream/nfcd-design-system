Warm-paper surface for grouping content — course listings, programme blocks, blog cards.

```jsx
<Card accent>
  <Eyebrow>Taller</Eyebrow>
  <h3>El cuerpo que escucha</h3>
  <p>Tres encuentros en Campo Arroyo del Medio.</p>
</Card>
```

`accent` adds the 3px wine top border; `raised` gives a lifted paper tone with a soft shadow. Default is a flat hairline-bordered card.
